Eat N' Park

Menu Design

This piece was all about utilizing typography and learning how layouts work. We have all been to restaurants and have had those menus that keep our eyes attracted to it, I wanted to do something for Eat n' Park since they are a Pittsburgh only thing. I wanted to create a menu that was pleasant to look at and utilizes Eat n' Parks main colors and keep it unique. It was a lot of fun to find fonts that worked with the logo and keep the flow of the menu without being too distracting.
