Elgato

Postcards and 3D Package

This project was an interesting one for me. It was my first time doing dielines and designing a flat box for what it would look like when it was printed and folded. I also had fun designing postcards that would grab the readers attention and not be the postcards that as soon as you grab them from the mailbox, they're in the garbage can. I mainly used the colors that I saw on their website since it's a nice blue and I liked looking at the color so I wanted to use that to my advantage.
