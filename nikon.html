Nikon

Billboard Tech Illustration Advertisement

This was a fun project to work on. Doing a tech illustration was something that I have never done before and I was happy with the way it turned out. I wanted to utilize the tech illustration for an advertisement. I decided to go with a billboard for a local tech store that would advertise the Nikon camera. The goal was to use artwork and color to grab your attention and make you take that exit and go to Bernie's and purchase a Nikon camera.
