Pure Hockey

Social Media Advertisement

This project was a lot of fun. I grew up a hockey fan so being able to combine two of my hobbies together was a blast. I wanted to go with a simple design that was appealing to the eye. With social media becoming the more dominant advertising channel, I wanted to create an ad for it and learn what works and what doesn't with social media. The main focus for this advertisement was to put Pure Hockey's name out there so they could potentially sponsor some peewee teams or school teams for hockey.
