Shop412

Gatefold Brochure

This was one of the pieces that got me out of my comfort zone. I have never done a brochure before and I didn't know how the layout worked or anything like that. I wanted to use Shop412 for this project since not tha many people know about them and I think a brochure would be a good way for people to get to know this company and potentially become a customer. I wanted the brochure to show off some of Shop412s products and let their colors pop out while staying true to Pittsburgh.
