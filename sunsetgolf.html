Sunset Golf

Logo and Brand Redesign

This campaign was all about finding what I want to do with my career. I've always been interested in apparel and wanted to get involved with the design and production aspect of it. I wanted to redesign Sunsets logo since the old logo was all over the place. I wanted to promote a local minigolf and driving range that is near me and supplied me with many good memories.
