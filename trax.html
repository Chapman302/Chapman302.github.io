Trax Farms

Brand Redesign

This piece was a lot of fun to work on. I combined different projects of things that I loved creating to come up with a campaign for Trax Farms. I wanted to create something for a local business that I loved going to. I would always go to Trax Farms and check out the crops they had growing then would end my trip with a nice apple cider slushie. I wanted to highlight some key things that Trax could do to advertise their products and their company to really get their name out there!
